4.15. How to manage enumerations?

To manage enumerations, click on the Enumerations button in the toolbar to display the list of existing enumerations (see Figure 4.1-2). You can then perform various Actions :

  • add a new enumeration (see Section 4.16),
  • edit enumeration (see Section 4.17).
  • reset enumerations to default value (see Figure 4.15-2).
